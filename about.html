Our Story

Celebrating life's special moments with creativity and passion

Creating Unforgettable Experiences

Founded in 2018, Ashel Events has grown from a small event planning service to a full-scale event management and gifting company. Our journey began with a simple mission: to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories through thoughtful design and impeccable execution.

What started as a passion project has blossomed into a thriving business that has helped hundreds of clients celebrate life's most important milestones.
